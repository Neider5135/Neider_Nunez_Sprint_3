About us

Get to know us better!

We are a family of ice cream makers passionate about our work. We love creating delicious and unique ice creams that reflect the passion of our customers. We have a different perspective on ice cream making. Instead of limiting ourselves to traditional flavors, we are inspired by the things our customers love. This has led us to create ice creams with a variety of themes, such as movie-themed ice cream, video game-themed ice cream, music-themed ice cream, sports-themed ice cream, and more.

Our ice creams are made with high-quality ingredients, fresh and natural. We offer a wide variety of flavors, from the most traditional to the most innovative. We are proud to be a family-owned business. We strive to provide personalized service and an unforgettable experience to our customers.

Our goal is to be the most innovative ice cream shop. We want to offer our customers ice creams that are more than just a meal, but an experience that makes them smile. We also want to expand to other countries. We believe that our concept of themed ice creams is unique and has the potential to be successful around the world.
